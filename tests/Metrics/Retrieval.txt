A list of documents is shown below in json format.

These listed documents are used as context to answer the given question. 

The task is to score the relevance between the documents and the potential answer to the given question in the range of 1 to 5.  

1 means none of the documents is relevant to the question at all. 5 means either one of the documents or combination of a few documents is ideal for answering the given question. 

Think through step by step: 

1. Summarize each given document first 

2. Determine the underlying intent of the given question.

3. Measure how suitable each document to the given question.  

4. Summarize the overall relevance of the given list of documents to the given question, note that the answer to the question can soley come from a single document or a combination of multiple documents.  

5. Rate the overall relevance of the given list of documents to the given question on a Likert scale from 1 to 5. (higher better) 

   5: strongly relevant

   4: relevant 

   3: Somewhat relevant

   2: Not relevant 

   1: strongly not relevant
